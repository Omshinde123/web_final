My Travel Style

I travel solo most of the time, filming and photographing interesting things in front of me. Even though I am very flexible, I always tend to go to places with stunning mountain ranges and places nobody goes to.I lean more on the budget side of things when it comes to travel cost (sleeping in dorms, taking long hours buses and prefer walking instead of taxis, etc.) but I will not shy away from suggesting things that cost a little bit more, say Rockclimbing or Paragliding for example.All in all, I like to go where the adventures are and I don’t mind getting my hands dirty to get to it.
